Pharah takes the action to skies, armed with a rocket launcher she barrages down high-explosive rockets. She removes the style of boots on the ground gameplay and make other players keep a keen eye on the skies.

Passive Abilities

Hover Jets

Pharah lifts herself upward with her jetpack, it allows her to gain height by holding either the jump or the alt-fire and tapping it allows her to remain at the same height. It refills automatically when it isn't being use taking only two seconds to refill from empty to full.

Weapon

Rocket Launcher

Pharah primary weapon shoots out rockets that deal damages in a A.O.E radius or the rocket can hit the enemy directly dealing massive damage compared to the A.O.E damage. (There is knockback to can also affct herself).

Jump Jet

Pharah propells herself by suit's thruster which lets her gain a maximum range of 20 meters vertically with a 10 secs cooldown.

Concussive Blast

Pharah shoots on a small rocket to doesn't do damage but instead knockbacks the enemies and can be used on herself. This can be also be used as a source of mobility as she can reposition herself and her enemies.

Barrage

Pharah controls a continous barrage of mini rockets to reign from above on her foes. When using her ultimate she remains stationary but can turn and hover in the air if it was used mid-air.
