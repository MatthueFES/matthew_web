Reaper is one of the offense characters in Overwatch. With his hellfire shotguns, the ability to become ammune to damage and the power to teleport within the shadows makes him one of the deadiliest characters in the earth and in game.

Passive Abilities

The reaping

Reaper steals health from his enemies as he damages them. The healing comes from 20% of hero damage dealt using his kit like his HellFire Shotguns, Death Blossom, or melee.

Wepaon

Hellfire Shotguns

Reaper tears apart his enemies with his twin shotguns.

Stats
Type: Shotgun Damage: 2-7 per bullet Falloff range: 11 to 20 meters Number of pellets: 20 per shot Rate of Fire: 2 shots per second Ammo: 8 Reload time: 1.5 seconds

Abilities

Wraith Form

Reaper becomes a shadow for a short time. In his wraith form he becomes invulnerable and is able to pass through enemies, but can't shoot or use any of his other abilities

Shadow Step

After Reaper marks a destination with the shadow, Reaper disappears and then reappears at that marked location.

Death Blossom

In a blur of motion, Reaper use his hellfire shotguns at imense speed, dealing insane damage to nearby enemies. When activated, Reaper shoots his shotguns in a large A.O.E (Area of Effect) around him, dealing mass damage to nearby enemies.
