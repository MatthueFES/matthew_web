Tracer

Tracer is a offensive hero on Overwatch that uses twin pulse pistols and pulse bombs to quickly burst down enemies. She is able to blink through space and rewind her own personal timeline to catch her enemies offguard.





















Weapon

Pulse Pistols

Tracer shoots both pistol atht deals heavy burst damage. Her pistols have a cone of fire and damage falloff making them most effective at close range.

  • Tracer's pulses pistol has a high rate of fire, which means a high recoil. Make sure you engage in close combat
  • Since they high rate of fire it's a good idea to know what you're shooting at. If you're shooting at enemies that can gain ult charge make sure to stop.

Abilities

Blink

This ability is one of most important features of tracer. Blink allows tracer to teleport over a short distance in the direction she is moving. (the maximum charge of blinks is 3.) This ablility allows her to move unpredictable able to confuse her enemies giving a massive advantage.

  • A good tip is to make sure where you blinking into, it's a good idea to have map awareness otherwise you might blink into a wall and wont understand why you weren't moving.
  • Using multiple blinks at the same time can allow tracer to cross chokepoints or large gaps without trouble

Recall

When activated, Tracer will rewind to three seconds in the past, Setting her HP and position at that time. it also reloads her pistol and removes debuff.

  • Recall is usually a way to restore hp back from combat, but can be a great way to bait enemies to your teammates or to outplay them.
  • When tracer is in animation, she will become invisible and invicible for that time.

Pulse Bomb

Tracer throws a bomb that sticks on any surface if thrown on it. After a couple of seconds, the bomb explodes dealing explosive damages.

  • Pulse Bomb is devastating on grouped up enemies or defenses like Bastion under Sentry mode.
  • Pulse Bomb synergizes very well with abilities such as Zarya's Graviton Surge. The combination of these ultimates will usually wipe out the majority of an enemies team and severely damage their tanks.
  • Quick tip: Be careful not to kill yourself with your bomb. You're not safe from your own bomb!
